Rules of the game

HOW TO PLAY

01

Set Up the Board

Place the Tilt Toss platform on any flat surface. The board pivots freely on its center base — that's what makes every round unpredictable.

02

Toss Your Bags

Players take turns tossing bean bags onto the board. Land one in the center box for max points — but watch out, every bag that lands changes the tilt.

03

Bags Stay, Score Points

Bags that stay on the board score. Bags in the box score bonus points. Bags that slide or knock others off — that's the chaos that makes Tilt Toss addictive.

04

First to 21 Wins

Tally your scores each round. First team to reach 21 points takes the crown — then immediately demands a rematch.
